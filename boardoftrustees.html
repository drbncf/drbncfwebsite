The trust is headed by 10 trustees with its registered office located in Mumbai.

SHRI. BINOD KUMAR CHOUBEY

Managing trustee / Chairman

Shri. Binod Kumar Choubey was working with the I.R.S in the Ministry of Finance, Govt. of India. He took voluntary retirement and devited his time for the social work activities undertaken by Dr. B. N. Choubey Foundation. He is also associated with other social organisations working in the field of social welfare.

SMT. SHANTI DEVI CHOUBEY

Mrs. Shanti Devi, Wife of Dr. B. N. Choubey is a symbol of the sacrifices she has made in her life. She has always supported her husband in everry social work activity he had undertaken. She was the motivator in setting up the trust to carry out welfare activities in an organised manner. We regularly recieve her guidance and will continue to do so for years to come, to ensure the growth of the trust, which in turn will ensure that the social welfare activities can be taken up effectively in order to fulfill Dr. B. N. Choubey's dream.

Dr. SAROJ KUMAR CHOUBEY

Dr. Saroj Kumar Choubey, one of the trustees is working as an advocate in the Patna High Court. He is a great social work activist and has devoted most of his time for social welfare activities undertaken by Dr. B. N. Choubey Foundation
