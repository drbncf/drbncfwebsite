Our Mission

The main objective of the trust is to work in the field of

  • Education
  • Health
  • Sports
  • Medical facilities and health camps
  • Ambulance servicies
  • Promotion of arts, crafts and handicrafts
  • Scholarships and free study materials to the needy students irrespective of caste, religion etc.
  • Education to girls and women
  • Providing aid during times of natural calamities like flood, fire, landslide etc. and any other emergencies
  • Help in the implementation of government welfare schemes

"Education and good health to all"

-Dr. Barmeshwar Nath Choubey
